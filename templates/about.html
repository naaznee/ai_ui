How to use

  1. Click choose files to open a diaglog that will let you upload your image(s)
  2. Select the image that you want to analyze and click open.
  3. Then click on the Process button in blue, to upload your images for processing.
  4. Be patient while we analyze the image(s)
  5. Once the analysis is done You should see the table of results with a thumbnail of the original image also with the age, gender and ethnicity estimation.
